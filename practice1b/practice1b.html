Cat The domestic cat (Felis silvestris catus or Felis catus) is a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal.
They are often called house cats when kept as indoor pets or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them from other felids and felines.
Cats are often valued by humans for companionship and for their ability to hunt vermin. (...) Fun facts about cats Owning a cat can reduce the risk of stroke and heart attack by a third.
